How it works

Step 01

Send one 835 export

Paperwork first: a mutual NDA and Business Associate Agreement before any file moves — we treat your data like the covered entity you are. Then your biller exports the remittance files (835/ERA) from the clearinghouse; every major one has the button.

Step 02

Get your Denial Opportunity Analysis in 48 hours

A client-ready report: denied dollars by payer and by category, the portion that's genuinely appealable, and your ten highest-value targets — each with the argument we'd make. The analysis runs entirely offline against your files. Keep the report either way.

Step 03

Pilot for 90 days, paid only from recoveries

Our engine drafts each appeal with citations into your clinical record — a letter that can't validate can't be approved. A named person on your staff reviews, approves, and files. When the payer pays, the recovery is matched automatically from the next remittance, so the results report writes itself.

No letter files itself.

Every appeal carries a Certification of Human Review — who approved it, when, and what the automated validation verified — bound into the filed packet. Drafting is PHI-minimized, a Business Associate Agreement comes before any file moves, and SOC 2 Type I preparation is underway. Built for specialty practices nationwide — including the state gold-card prior-auth exemptions your payers won't mention.
